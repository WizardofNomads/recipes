Grandma's Iced Lemon Bundt Cake

Photo credit: from this recipe blog post with an alternative method for this dish

Recipe Description

This was a dessert you would find at any family gathering. It was great for anytime of year but for me it carries extra fondness around the holiday season. It is a simple recipe but is tricky to get the icing just right.

Ingredients

  • Yellow Cake Mix
  • Eggs
  • Lemon
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. This simple receipe just comes from a box cake mix but it is still very tasty. Open the mix into a large bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs in the mix until well mixed.
  3. Grease bundt pan mold and pour mix into the mold. Bake at the correct temperature for appropriate amount of time.
  4. Allow the cake to cool on a wire rack after removing from the mold. Wait to apply the icing until the cake has also had a chance to cool.
  5. Prepare the icing by mixing powdered sugar with lemon juice until acheiving a frosty white color and thick consistency. Drizzle the icing along the top of the bundt cake and let the frosting drip down the sides.
